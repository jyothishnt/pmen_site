News

  • The 9th PMEN meeting was held in Alice Springs, Australia during ISPPD-5 in April 2006. New clones were included into the Network and details can be found at Clone Collection
  • The PMEN has decided to include major invasive antibiotic-susceptible clones that have a wide geographic spread. Potential candidate clones and information can be submitted to curator Lesley McGee.
  • The 10th PMEN meeting is scheduled for the 6th International Symposium on Pneumococci and Pneumococcal Disease (ISPPD) which will take place in Reykjavik in Iceland from June 9th to 12th, 2008.
